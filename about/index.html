Silvin Homes Team

Jay Lococo

Founder of Silvin Homes

I've been working in construction for ten years now, focusing mostly on building new homes and renovating existing ones. Over this time, I've developed a solid understanding of what it takes to bring a project from an idea to reality. I take pride in doing things the right way and making sure my clients are satisfied with the results.

Every project is a chance to create something meaningful for someone, and that's what keeps me going. I'm more about letting the work speak for itself, knowing that the details and the care put into each job matter most. My approach is simple: listen to what people want and work hard to make it happen. It's a journey of constant learning and improvement, and I'm grateful for the trust my clients have shown me.

Craig Nolan

Project Manager

Craig brings over 10 years of project management experience in residential construction and development. Known for his strong communication skills, attention to detail, and organizational leadership, Craig ensures every project runs smoothly from start to finish. His hands-on approach and commitment to excellence make him a trusted partner for homeowners, subcontractors, and the Silvin Homes team.

As a dedicated member of Silvin Homes, Craig plays a key role in coordinating schedules, managing vendors, and overseeing day-to-day operations — ensuring that every home we build meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
