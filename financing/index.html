Construction Financing Expertise

John joined Elm Tree Funding in 2023 with a heavy focus on Construction loans and Construction-to-Permanent loans. With an extensive background in the industry, he has a deep understanding of the unique challenges and opportunities that arise with building a new home. His expertise and personalized approach make him a trusted advisor for clients seeking tailored solutions for their construction financing needs.

John is an active member of the Home Builders & Remodelers Association of Connecticut (HBRA) and was recognized for his success by winning a HOBI award for best Construction Permanent Custom Home Loan for 2023. In his spare time you can find him hanging out with family or out on the golf course. He looks forward to helping your family finance and build the home of your dreams.
